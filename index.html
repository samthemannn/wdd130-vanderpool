Dinosaur Hips

Dinosaurs come in two main classifications. These classifications are based on the hip structure of the skeleton.

Two Main Classifications

This site provides descriptions and comparisons of the dinosaur hip structures that define saurischians and ornithischians.

Ornithischians

Bird-hipped dinosaurs include triceratops, ankylosaurus, and stegosaurus.

Saurischians

Lizard-hipped dinosaurs include Archaeopteryx, T. rex, Brontosaurus, and modern birds.
