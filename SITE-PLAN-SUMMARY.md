## Homepage Content

- Dinosaurs come in two main classifications.
- The classifications are based on skeleton hip structure.
- Ornithischians means "bird hipped."
- Saurischians means "lizard hipped."
- Include the ornithischian and saurischian pelvis images.

## Ornithischians Page Content

- "Ornithischia" means "bird hip."
- Ornithischians were typically herbivorous or omnivorous.
- Popular examples include triceratops, ankylosaurus, and stegosaurus.
- Explain the ilium, pubis, and ischium bones.
- Note that ornithischians are extinct after the K-Pg mass extinction.

## Saurischians Page Content

- "Saurischia" means "lizard hip."
- Carnivorous dinosaurs are classified as saurischians, along with two lines of herbivorous sauropodomorphs.
- Popular examples include Archaeopteryx, T. rex, and Brontosaurus.
- Explain the ilium, pubis, and ischium bones.
- Modern saurischians include birds such as cassowaries, secretary birds, and harriers.
